# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Koncat handles information when you use the service.

## Information We Collect

We collect account information such as your name, email address, authentication details, team memberships, and settings.

We also process project information, including project names, deployments, files, pages, access settings, and usage events needed to operate and secure the service.

## How We Use Information

We use information to provide the service, authenticate users, publish and serve pages, enforce access controls, prevent abuse, troubleshoot issues, improve reliability, and communicate with you about your account.

## Published Content

Content published as public can be viewed by anyone with access to the link and may be indexed or shared by others. Password, magic link, and private pages are limited by the access controls you configure.

## Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us host, operate, secure, and support Koncat, or when required by law.

## Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may delete projects or request account-related changes by contacting us.

## Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No system is perfectly secure, so you should avoid publishing sensitive content unless the selected access controls are appropriate for your use case.

## Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to hello@koncat.co.
